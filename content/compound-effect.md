The compound effect is a fundamental principle that underlies [[atomic-habits]] and most personal development.

> Consistency over intensity.

## Key Ideas

- 1% better every day = 37x better in a year
- Small choices don't feel significant in the moment
- Time is the amplifier

## Applications

This applies to learning, fitness, relationships, and wealth building.
